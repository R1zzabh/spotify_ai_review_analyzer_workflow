2. Workflow architecture

1IngestReviews from app stores, Reddit, Spotify Community, forums, X/Twitter, support tickets.
2NormalizeDeduplicate, remove spam, detect language, redact PII, split multi-issue feedback.
3LLM ExtractClassify pain, JTBD, segment, root cause, product surface, sentiment and severity.
4Cluster + RAGGroup related complaints and preserve traceable evidence snippets for each insight.
5PrioritizeScore opportunities using frequency, severity, segment value, confidence and effort.
